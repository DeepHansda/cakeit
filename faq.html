FAQ



  • What quality cakes will be delivered to us?

    • We have a team of best Cake artists, Chefs in town. We can ensure that our customers will never have to complain regarding quality of cakes.

  • Can we choose delivery time and date?

    • Yes, you can choose delivery date and time from 6AM to midnight. We charge extra delivery charges for midnight delivery.

  • Do you deliver fresh cakes?

    • Cakes are manufactured after we get order from your side. So, all the cakes are delivered fresh for you to enjoy the real taste of the cakes.

  • Is same day delivery available?

    • No, as the cakes are completely customized and we wish to give you the best product and services. So, we opt for 24 hrs delivery time.

  • When can I cancel my order and how?

    • You can cancel your order 24 hours before delivery time. After that, order cancellation requests will not be entertained. If you placed an order whose delivery time is within 24 hours of order placing time then order can't be cancelled and no refund will be processed for the same. For cancelling your order, you need to send us an email on cakee.it1@gmail.com with the order id from the same email address which you have placed that order.

  • How many days it takes for refund?

    • It will take 1-2 business days after your cancellation request is sent.

  • Is it necessary to have an account on Cakee it to shop products?

    • Yes, it is quite easy to create your account on Cakee it. Also, it is recommendable to create your account on Cakee it. This will help you to personalize your experience and get the benefit of our special offers available for regular customers.

  • What payment options are available on Cakee it?

    • You can pay through multiple payment options like Credit card, Debit Card, Net Banking, UPI.
